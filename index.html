Lim Campus

Lim Campus, the newest addition to our educational network, was established in January 2025. Nestled in a serene 8-hectare area, Lim Campus has quickly become a prominent institution in the region. Starting with an initial enrollment of 500 students, it now proudly serves over 4,000 students.

Lim Campus is dedicated to providing high-quality education and continuously strives to exceed the expectations of its students, parents, and the community. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our state-of-the-art facilities and comprehensive programs designed to foster academic and personal growth.
